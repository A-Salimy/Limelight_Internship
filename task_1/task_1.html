You play as Lee Everett, a convicted criminal who gets a second chance when the zombie apocalypse begins.

After a car crash, Lee meets Clementine, an 8-year-old girl searching for her missing parents.

Lee takes her under his wing, and together they join a group of survivors seeking refuge, and to find Clementine's parents.

The game features multiple endings based on player choices throughout the episodes.

Your decisions affect relationships with other characters and story outcomes.
